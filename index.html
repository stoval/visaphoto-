How it works

Three steps.
That's it.

01

Choose your document type

Select from passport, visa, or ID card. Pick your country to load the exact size requirements automatically.

02

Upload your photo

Take or upload a portrait photo. The tool will remove the background, crop to the correct size, and adjust the framing.

03

Download and print

Export a single image or a printable sheet. Bring to any photo print shop or print at home.

Your photos stay on your device

VisaPics runs entirely in your browser using local processing. We have no server, no account system, and no way to access your photos — because they never leave your device. What you see is what you get.
